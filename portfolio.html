Using replit to make Higher/Lower game.

This is our Higher/Lower game. A game where you can choose any maximum value you want. You then have to guess a number in that range. The game will keep telling you if your guess is higher or lower untill you get to the desired digit. There is also a scoreboard where you can see yourself compared to other people.


Using Replit to make an E-card.

We used replit to make an E-card for the sick children in the hospital. When you open the card there is a "Get Well" message at the top. There is also a minion that starts dancing with music playing in the background. Then a few balloons appear and move around.
